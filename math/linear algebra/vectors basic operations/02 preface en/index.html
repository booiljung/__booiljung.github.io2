This book aims to systematically cover the basic concepts of vectors, starting from their fundamental definitions to various operations and their geometric interpretations. Vectors are essential tools used in fields such as mathematics, physics, and engineering, and the purpose of this book is to aid in a foundational understanding of vectors.

The book is structured to help readers easily grasp the concepts of vectors and apply them to real-world problems. It begins with the definition and geometric representation of vectors, then moves on to vector addition and subtraction, scalar multiplication, vector products, dot and cross products, and other important concepts. Each topic is accompanied by clear explanations and geometric insights. Furthermore, the book delves into more advanced topics, such as vector orthogonality, projection, linear combinations, and coordinate transformations, providing a comprehensive understanding of how vectors can be applied in various contexts.

This book is intended for readers who already have a basic knowledge of vectors. Each chapter includes practice problems, allowing readers to review and apply what they have learned. Through this book, I hope readers gain a deeper understanding of vectors and develop the ability to solve practical problems.

The world of vectors plays a crucial role in mathematical principles and real-life problem solving. I hope this book serves as a valuable resource for your studies and that you master the principles and applications of vectors, which will greatly benefit your future academic and research endeavors.
