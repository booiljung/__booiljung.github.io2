Dear Readers,

Thank you for opening this book. Given your background in both mathematics and programming, you are likely aware of the pivotal role Kalman filters play in signal processing and system estimation. This book is dedicated to an in-depth exploration of advanced Kalman filters, focusing exclusively on their theoretical foundations and practical applications without delving into variants such as the Extended Kalman Filter or Adaptive Kalman Filter.

Kalman filters are powerful tools for estimating the state of a system that evolves over time, particularly excelling in scenarios involving linear systems. Advanced Kalman filters build upon these fundamental principles, offering refined mathematical techniques and programming approaches to enhance state estimation accuracy. My goal is to provide a clear and comprehensive understanding of these sophisticated filters and to offer insights into their application in real-world problems.

Each chapter systematically explains the theoretical background, clarifies mathematical details, and includes practical code examples to facilitate understanding. We will delve into complex problem-solving algorithms and optimization techniques, equipping you with the knowledge to apply Kalman filters effectively to advanced scenarios.

I hope this book will not only deepen your understanding of advanced Kalman filters but also empower you to tackle complex problems with greater confidence. May it serve as a valuable resource in your ongoing journey of learning and application in this dynamic field.

Thank you for your interest and engagement.

Sincerely,
