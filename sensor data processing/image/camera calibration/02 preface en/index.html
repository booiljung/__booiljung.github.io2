Camera calibration is an essential process in today's rapidly evolving technological landscape. From autonomous vehicles to augmented reality and robotic vision systems, high-precision image processing is increasingly required, and achieving this level of accuracy depends on proper camera calibration. This book delves into the principles and methods of camera calibration, providing readers with practical knowledge that can be directly applied in real-world scenarios.

While the calibration process may seem complex at first, it becomes approachable with a solid understanding of the theoretical background and the right tools. In particular, this book offers hands-on tutorials using OpenCV, MATLAB, and Python to guide readers in implementing camera calibration and optimizing performance in real applications.

Camera calibration is not just a technical step but a key that unlocks the future of vision-based systems. With precise calibration, more accurate and efficient systems become possible, opening up countless opportunities that will enrich our lives. We hope this book will be a valuable resource for you, and we are excited to accompany you on this journey of learning and discovery.

We wish you great success in mastering camera calibration and applying it to your projects!
