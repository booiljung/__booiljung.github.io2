The importance of the roles and responsibilities assigned to each member is crucial for the successful operation and performance improvement of an organization. Clear role division and responsibility allocation within an organization enable efficient collaboration and communication, providing a foundation for quick responses when issues arise. In particular, confusion regarding roles and responsibilities can lead to conflicts within the organization, negatively affecting overall performance.

Therefore, by clarifying roles and responsibilities, we aim to contribute to the achievement of organizational goals and offer ways to positively influence organizational culture and individual growth. This topic is especially significant in understanding and applying organizational theory, and we hope that through this content, readers will be able to drive tangible changes in their organizations.
